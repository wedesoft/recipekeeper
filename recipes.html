Cranberry Glaze

Courses: Dressing
Categories: Cranberry, Decoration, Fruit, Orange, Sauce, Sugar
Collections:
Source:
Serving size: 2 1/3 cups
Preparation time:
Cooking time:

Ingredients

12 oz Cranberries, fresh

1 c Sugar

1 c Water

1 1/2 ts Orange peel, grated

Directions

1. Combine cranberries and sugar in processor.

2. Using on/off turns, coarsely chop cranberries.

3. Transfer mixture to heavy medium saucepan.

4. Add 1 cup water and orange peel and bring to boil, stirring occasionally.

5. Reduce heat simmer until mixture is reduced to 2 1/3 cups, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes.

6. Cool to room temperature.

7. Cover chill overnight or up to 2 days (mixture will thicken).

Cream Delight

Courses: Dessert, Dressing, Sauces, Dips, Condiments etc
Categories: Alcohol, Cream - Whipping, Dessert, Honey, Lemon, Nuts - Almond, Oatmeal, Snack
Collections:
Source:
Serving size: 6 Servings
Preparation time:
Cooking time:

Ingredients

2/3 c Oatmeal, uncooked

1/3 c Almonds, slivered

1 1/4 c Whipping cream

5 tb Honey, to taste

4 tb Irish whiskey

1 ts Lemon juice

Directions

1. On a baking sheet, toast the oatmeal and almonds at 150°C for 5 to 10 minutes, stirring frequently.

2. Whip the cream, but not too stiff.

3. Stir honey and whiskey into whipped cream.

4. Fold in almonds and oatmeal.

5. Stir in lemon juice.

6. Divide into individual long-stemmed glasses.

7. Serve at room temperature or chilled.

Cream Dressing for Cucumbers

Courses: Dressing, Sauces, Dips, Condiments etc
Categories: American, Cream - Sour, Cucumber, Dip, Dressing, Paprika, Salad, Vinegar
Collections:
Source:
Serving size: 1 Servings
Preparation time:
Cooking time:

Ingredients

1 Cucumber, large

1/2 c Sour cream

1 tb Vinegar

Paprika

Salt

Directions

1. Pare cucumber and slice very thin, place in bowl, add salt and place in the refrigerator.

2. After 2 hours, rinse thoroughly 2 or 3 times in ice cold water, pressing the cucumber slices between the hands each time to remove all salt.

3. After the last rinsing, add the following dressing: Combine the sour cream with the vinegar and paprika mix well.

4. If desired, chopped chives or sliced onions may be added.

Crumbled Danish Blue Cheese Dressing

Courses: Dressing, Sauces, Dips, Condiments etc, Snack, Starter
Categories: Cheese - Blue, Dressing, Lemon, Oil, Sauce, Snack, Starter, Sugar, Tabasco, Vinegar
Collections:
Source:
Serving size: 1 Cup
Preparation time:
Cooking time:

Ingredients

3/4 c Wine Vinegar

1 tb Salt

4 ts Sugar, heaped

1/2 ts Tabasco sauce

1 1/2 ts Lemon juice

1 c Salad oil

1/2 lb Blue cheese, Danish

Directions

1. Mix all ingredients except oil and cheese, until sugar and salt dissolve.

2. Blend in oil using a spoon or whisk, not a mixer or blender.

3. Crumble the cheese with a fork and fold in.

4. Before using dressing, stir gently.

5. Toss with washed and dried salad greens, preferable romaine.

Dill Cream Dressing

Courses: Dressing, Sauces, Dips, Condiments etc
Categories: Condiment, Cream - Sour, Dill, Marinade, Mayonnaise, Mustard, Salad, Sauce
Collections:
Source:
Serving size: 1 1/2 cups
Preparation time:
Cooking time:

Ingredients

1 c Mayonnaise

1/2 c Sour Cream -OR- Yoghurt, plain

2 ts Mustard dijon

1 tb Dill, finely chopped, fresh

Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

1. Beat together all ingredients and season well with salt and pepper.

2. More mustard and some lemon juice can be added to suit individual taste.

3. Milk or cream can be added for a thinner dressing.

4. Chill an hour or more before serving.
